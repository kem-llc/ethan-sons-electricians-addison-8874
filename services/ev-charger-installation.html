EV Charger Installation

Professional installation of Level 2 EV chargers for your home or business.

Schedule this service

Overview

Owning an electric vehicle means you need a reliable, fast charger at home. Our electricians install Level 2 chargers from top brands like Tesla, ChargePoint, and JuiceBox. We assess your panel capacity, run dedicated circuits, and mount the charger for optimal convenience. Plus, we help you take advantage of rebates and tax credits.

What's included

  • Faster charging than standard outlets
  • Increases home value and convenience
  • Eligible for federal and local incentives

How it works

1

Site Assessment

We inspect your panel, parking location, and existing wiring to determine the best setup.

2

Permitting & Rebates

We handle permits and help you apply for available rebates to offset costs.

3

Installation & Testing

We install the charger, run the circuit, and test the system to ensure safe, reliable charging.
