Panel Upgrades & Replacement

Upgrade your electrical panel for safety, capacity, and peace of mind.

Schedule this service

Overview

An outdated or undersized electrical panel is a fire hazard and can't handle modern power demands. Our team specializes in upgrading panels to 200 amps or more, ensuring your home meets current code and can support appliances, EV chargers, and electronics. We handle permits, inspections, and all utility coordination for a hassle-free experience.

What's included

  • Increases home safety and reduces fire risk
  • Supports modern appliances and electronics
  • Adds value to your property

How it works

1

Assessment

We evaluate your current panel, load requirements, and future needs to recommend the right upgrade.

2

Permitting

We pull all necessary permits and schedule inspections so you don't have to.

3

Installation

Our electricians install the new panel with clean wiring, label circuits, and test everything before turning power back on.
