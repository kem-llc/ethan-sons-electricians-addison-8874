Lighting Installation & Design

Custom lighting solutions for indoor and outdoor spaces, from accent to security lighting.

Schedule this service

Overview

Great lighting transforms your space. We design and install custom lighting systems that enhance ambiance, improve functionality, and increase security. From recessed lighting and chandeliers to landscape and motion-sensor lights, we bring your vision to life.

What's included

  • Enhanced aesthetics and curb appeal
  • Improved safety with well-lit pathways and entrances
  • Energy savings with LED and smart lighting
  • Increased home or business value

How it works

1

Design Consultation

We discuss your goals and preferences, and create a lighting plan that fits your style and budget.

2

Fixture Selection

We help you choose from a wide range of fixtures and bulbs, including energy-efficient options.

3

Professional Installation

Our electricians install all fixtures, wiring, and controls with precision and care.
