Common questions

How do I know if my panel needs upgrading?

Signs include frequent breaker trips, dimming lights, or using knob-and-tube wiring. If your home is over 25 years old, an inspection is recommended.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee and manufacturer warranties on parts.
