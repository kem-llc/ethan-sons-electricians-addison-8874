Whole-Home Rewiring

Replace old, unsafe wiring throughout your home.

Schedule this service

Overview

Homes with outdated wiring (knob-and-tube, aluminum, or degraded Romex) pose serious risks. We completely rewire your home to modern standards, ensuring safety and reliability.

What's included

  • Eliminates fire hazards
  • Supports modern electronics
  • Increases home value

How it works

1

Assessment

We inspect the existing wiring and create a detailed plan.

2

Rough-In

We run new cables, install boxes, and prepare for drywall repair.

3

Finish & Test

We connect fixtures, outlets, and switches, then test every circuit.
