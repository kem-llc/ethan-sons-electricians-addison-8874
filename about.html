Our story

Your Trusted Addison Electricians Since 1995

Our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services to the Addison community, treating every home and business as if it were our own.

Ethan & Sons Electricians was founded in 1995 by Master Electrician Ethan Miller with a simple vision: deliver exceptional electrical work with honesty and integrity. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a family-run business serving thousands of satisfied customers across Addison and the surrounding areas. We believe in building lasting relationships through quality workmanship and transparent communication.

Today, our team of licensed electricians continues that tradition. We invest in ongoing training and the latest tools to ensure we meet the evolving needs of our community. Whether it's a small repair or a large commercial project, we approach every job with the same commitment to excellence. We're proud to be your neighbors and your go-to electricians.
