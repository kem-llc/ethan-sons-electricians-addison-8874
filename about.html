Our story

Family-Owned & Committed to Excellence

To provide safe, reliable electrical services with integrity and transparency – treating every home like our own.

Ethan & Sons Electricians started in 1998 when founder Ethan Miller saw the need for honest, high-quality electrical work in Addison. With a small truck and a big dream, he built a reputation for showing up on time, fixing problems thoroughly, and never cutting corners. Over the years, his sons joined the business, bringing new skills and a shared passion for serving the community that raised them.

Today, we're a team of licensed electricians who take pride in our 4.8-star rating and over 500 glowing reviews. We invest in ongoing training, use the latest tools, and treat every customer like family. Whether it's a simple outlet repair or a full home rewiring, we bring the same dedication to every job – because your safety and satisfaction are our legacy.
